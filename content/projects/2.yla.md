A residential management application designed to streamline daily and monthly tasks for StarCity Living residents. The platform enables residents to manage bill payments, schedule maintenance, view announcements, and book community facilities including futsal, tennis, basketball, and badminton courts.

## Key Features

- Separate applications for residents, merchants, and operations teams
- Coin-based reward system with purchase and redemption capabilities
- Community feed with posts, photos, and hashtags
- Facility reservation booking system
- Multiple campaign types — games, stamps, surveys, quizzes, referral programs, raffles
