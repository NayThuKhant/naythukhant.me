A mobile banking application serving Hana Microfinance Limited, a South Korean financial group subsidiary. The platform connects customers, field officers, and management teams to a core banking system (Finflux) for loan management, supporting both offline and online operation for field personnel in areas with limited connectivity.

## Key Features

- Dedicated apps for customers, field officers, and operational staff
- Offline-first architecture with synchronization for field use
- Comprehensive loan, disbursement, repayment, and interest management
- Processes ~1 million financial records monthly across 380K+ users
