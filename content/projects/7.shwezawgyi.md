A comprehensive pharmacy management system delivered as a SaaS product. Shwe Zawgyi supports multi-branch pharmacy chains with point-of-sale, inventory management, vendor management, and business analytics — all accessible from a single dashboard.

## Key Features

- Multi-branch and cashier management
- Point of Sale (POS) with inventory tracking
- Vendor management system
- Analytics dashboard for business insights
