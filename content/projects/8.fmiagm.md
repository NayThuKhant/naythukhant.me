A virtual meeting platform built for Yoma Strategic Holdings Ltd, a prominent Myanmar business group. The application enables shareholders to participate in Annual General Meetings remotely, cast votes on resolutions, and access meeting reports with automated result calculation based on shareholder votes and share quantities.

## Key Features

- Live meeting and discussion streaming
- Resolution voting with automated tallying
- Shareholder reports and result breakdowns
