An HR management platform derived from Better HR that streamlines personnel operations for Yoma Strategic Holdings Ltd. The system consolidates employee data, scheduling, and payroll functions into a unified interface available on web, Android, and iOS.

## Key Features

- Internal social media, messaging, and video calling
- Employee tracking, work hours, and overtime administration
- Leave management and check-in/check-out functionality
- Payslip and payroll processing
- Administrative form management
